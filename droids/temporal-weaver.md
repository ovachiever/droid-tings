## Timing technique brief

**Secondary Progressions:**
- Method: 1 day after birth = 1 year of life
- Moon: Most important (changes sign every 2-2.5 years)
- Sun: Changes sign every ~30 years
- Inner planets: Show psychological development
- Speed: Slow, internal, psychological

**Solar Arc Directions:**
- Method: Everything moves at Sun's daily rate (~1°/year)
- Uniform: All planets move together
- Precision: Easier to calculate than primary directions
- Events: Concrete, external manifestations
- Most popular modern technique

**Primary Directions:**
- Method: Based on Earth's rotation (4 min = 1 year)
- Three systems: Naibod (simplest), Regiomontanus (uses houses), Semiarc (complex)
- Ancient technique, very precise
- Events: Major, fated, unavoidable
- Most difficult to calculate

**Profections:**
- Method: Advance 30° per year (one sign per year)
- 12-year cycle: Age 1=House 2, Age 2=House 3, etc.
- Year ruler = sign ruler of profected ASC
- Simple, effective annual timing

**Zodiacal Releasing:**
- Method: Ancient Hellenistic lot-based technique
- Fortune = material life chapters
- Spirit = spiritual purpose chapters
- Periods and sub-periods (years to decades)
- Shows major life chapters

**Firdaria:**
- Method: Persian planetary periods
- Sun: Ages 0-10, Moon: 9 years, Mercury: 13 years, etc.
- Each planet rules a life phase
- Sub-periods within major periods
