## Antiscia calculation

To calculate antiscia:
- **Antiscia formula**: 30° - longitude in sign (Cancer/Capricorn axis)
- Example: 15° Gemini antiscia = 15° Cancer
- **Contra-antiscia formula**: Opposite sign, same degree
- Example: 15° Gemini contra-antiscia = 15° Sagittarius

## Parallel calculation

Declination ranges:
- **Parallel**: Two planets within ±1° same declination
- **Contra-parallel**: Two planets ±1° opposite declination (across equator)
- **Out-of-bounds**: Declination > ±23.5° (beyond Sun's path)
