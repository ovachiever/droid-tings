## Orb considerations

**Conjunction orbs for fixed stars:**
- 1st magnitude stars: ±2°
- 2nd magnitude stars: ±1°
- 3rd-4th magnitude: ±0.5°
- Royal Stars: ±2° (some say ±5° for life themes)
- Galactic Center: ±2-3°

**Tightest orbs most powerful:**
- ±0.5° = extremely powerful, life-defining
- ±1° = very strong influence
- ±2° = noticeable throughout life

## Precession note

Fixed stars precess 1° every 72 years:
- Aldebaran was 8° Gemini in 1900
- Now at 10° Gemini (2025)
- Will be 11° Gemini in 2100

Always check star position for birth year!

## Parans (Brady's technique)

Stars rising/setting simultaneously with planets:
- Heliacal rising (star rises with Sun)
- Acronychal rising (star rises as Sun sets)
- Angular contacts (star on angles when planet on angle)
- Adds layer of meaning beyond simple conjunction
